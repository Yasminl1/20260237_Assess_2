Information that beginners should know

Before joining Siva Samoa’s classes, beginners should come prepared with the appropriate attire and a few simple expectations in mind. Students are encouraged to wear an ‘ie lavalava during practice (‘ie lavalava is a traditional piece of material worn around the waist by both men and women). If you’re unsure how to wear it, instructors will be there to guide you and assist in tying it correctly. It is preferable that your hair is tied up to keep it near and out of the way while dancing.  For extra support, you can scroll down on the ‘Classes’ page to find a helpful video that shows how to wear an ‘ie lavalava and prepare for your first session.
