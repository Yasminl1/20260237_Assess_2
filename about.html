About

Siva Samoa is a website and organisation dedicated to teaching and sharing knowledge about traditional Samoan female dance. It aims to educate people about the cultural meaning, history, and movements of Siva Samoa in an accessible and respectful way. The organisation also offers beginner-friendly classes, making it easy for people of all ages and experience levels to learn basic steps, posture, and hand movements. Through its teaching, Siva Samoa helps preserve Samoan culture while encouraging confidence, expression, and connection to tradition through dance.
